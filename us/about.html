Francisco Borré

Graphic Designer • Motion Designer • UX/UI Designer

The journey until now

Currently a Graphic Design Technologist with an MBA in Digital Product Design / UX-UI Design.

Since I was young, I had a passion for drawing and creating mangas, which naturally led me to the Design universe. In 2017, I joined the ranks of the Brazilian Army, working as a designer and videomaker under the command of the 15th Mechanized Infantry Brigade. During this period, I honed my skills in video editing and storytelling.

After three years of military service, I began my academic journey in Graphic Design Technology, completed in 2021. Along the way, I collaborated with the Gruber business group, working for German Tech Systems (as a UX/UI designer and graphic designer), Gruber Accounting, and CRM Pro-Sales (managing social media). Additionally, I worked as a freelancer for companies such as Joy Street, Nell Bux, Pamela Gabriella Make Up, and Otimize Frete, developing projects in Branding, Visual Identity, Motion Design, and UI/UX Design.

In 2022, I delved deeper into research concepts and tools, prototyping, testing, and validation by completing an MBA in Digital Product Design - UX/UI. Currently, I am part of the Market Intelligence department at Prati-Donaduzzi, the largest tablet producer in Latin America. I work as a Marketing Analyst, focusing primarily on designing packaging for nutraceuticals and medications.

Furthermore, I am a proud father of a 5-year-old princess who teaches me every day to appreciate life with more curiosity and creativity.
